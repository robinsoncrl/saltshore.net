The SaltShore Suite

Built for freelancers and sole operators who are done losing money to disconnected apps, spreadsheets, and SaaS subscriptions that hold their data hostage.

Why the SaltShore Suite?

Standalone tools solve isolated problems. The SaltShore Suite is designed to maintain continuity across scheduling, finance, and records.

Shared principles across tools reduce surprises, edge cases, and re‑learning as workflows grow.

The pipeline

CalGen → FinPro → LedgerPro

Structured time → Defensible invoice → Reconciled record

Shared principles

  • Offline-first. No cloud dependency. No credential delegation. No account required.
  • One-time purchase. No subscription. No monthly fee. Your data is never held hostage.
  • Deterministic. Same input always produces the same output. Every figure is traceable to its source.
  • No silent failures. Every discrepancy is surfaced explicitly. Approximation is a bug, not a feature.
  • Local data ownership. Your records live on your machine in portable, open formats.

Individual tools

  • CalGen — Standards-first iCalendar generation and validation.
  • FinPro — Deterministic billing and reproducible financial artifacts.
  • LedgerPro — Deterministic statement-to-ledger normalization and reconciliation.
